Welcome to our blog. Here you will find articles about our projects, experiments and just lyrics. The blog was created as a platform in order to share experience primarily for the development team DigitalRats. Although the “team” is a little loudly said - now it consists of only one person.

Nevertheless, I hope that designers can take advantage of this magazine.

Publications can be short, like this one, more worthy of twitter or a Telegram channel, or very large and difficult to understand. How to read this? There will always be a simple static site, maybe I’ll bother with search, tag clouds and all the other fancy stuff someday, but it will not be soon (there are no databases and full-text search engines, no nodejs or PHP on the server side). I think if you are interested in what will happen next - just follow the publications. Someday, search engines will get to us, but for now just read the latest publication and continue to the past until you get bored.
